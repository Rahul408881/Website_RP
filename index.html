Brainstem Segmentation using UNET

Research Project

Segmentation and Recognition of brain stem from MRI scans using deep learning UNET model. Applied different masks and CNN models for testing and achieved model accuracy of ~93%

Parkinson's Disease Detection using ResNet50

Research Project

Built a ResNet architecture for prediction and recognition of hand-drawn images for prediction of onset of disease. Successfully generated models for various iterated epochs over different ratios of train-test splits. Prediction achieved at 96 percent with successful recognition of the hand-drawn image pattern.

Photo spectrometer for Chemical Analysis and Mass Spectroscopy for Covid Detection

Research Project

Designing a low-cost low-power portable photo spectrometer with advancements in power consumption and statistical analysis of the spectrum diffracted from the solution. Advancements involving mounting of the same on a puma robotic arm with mass spectrometry to analyze covid samples with a contactless approach.
